How sessions work

From vote to industry insight

01

Members Vote

Every session topic is put to a club-wide vote. Each member gets one vote — the curriculum is always what the group actually wants to learn.

02

We Prepare

The curriculum team builds materials, case studies, and discussion frameworks around the winning topic so sessions have real depth.

03

Speaker Session

An industry practitioner from the field joins to bridge theory and practice — sharing how the concept actually plays out in their career.

04

Open Discussion

Members engage directly with the speaker, ask questions, and debate real-world implications — building both knowledge and network.
