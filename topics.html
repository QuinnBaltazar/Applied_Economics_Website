Topics Covered

Our club dives into applied economics through market research, policy analysis, and practical finance applications. These topics help members build the skills they need for real-world economic careers.

Market Analysis

Explore equity trends, sector performance, and macro drivers to understand how financial markets behave.

Economic Policy

Understand policy impacts on growth, employment, and investment through real case studies and debate.

Data & Strategy

Use quantitative tools and research frameworks to evaluate decisions and build evidence-backed recommendations.
