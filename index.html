Our Mission

Applied Economics Club is dedicated to empowering ambitious students with practical economic insight and job-ready skills. We translate classroom learning into meaningful experiences, internships, and leadership opportunities so members can confidently pursue careers in consulting, finance, policy, and beyond.

From data-driven analysis to professional communication, our club helps members build the bridge from concept to impact.
