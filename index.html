Our Mission

Theory grounded in practice.

We teach a topic chosen by club vote, then bring in an industry speaker to show members exactly how that concept plays out in the real world — from venture capital and trading to wealth management and investment banking.

We also keep everyone connected after graduation. The Members page lets club members update their goals, share what they're working on, and reach out to one another so the network stays alive long after the diploma.

What We Do

Member-Driven Topics

Every session topic is put to a club-wide vote. The curriculum is always what members actually want to learn — not what someone decided years ago.

Industry Speakers

After each topic vote we bring in a practitioner from that field to bridge the gap between academic concepts and how markets really work.

Lasting Network

The verified member directory keeps alumni and current students connected long after graduation — a network built to stay active and useful.
