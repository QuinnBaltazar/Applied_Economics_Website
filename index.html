Applied Economics Club

Our Mission

Our mission is to take big ideas and ground them in reality through practical use. We teach a topic chosen by club vote, then bring in an industry speaker to show members how that concept works in the real world.

We also keep everyone connected after graduation. The Members page lets club members update their goals, share what they’re interested in, and contact one another so the network stays active.
